Our Story & Mission

The Definitive Voice of Coimbatore Real Estate

Setting the Blueprint for Excellence.

The Coimbatore Association of Realtors (COAREA) is a not-for-profit professional body dedicated to empowering, representing, and elevating real estate professionals across Coimbatore, Tirupur, Nilgiris, Erode, Salem, and neighbouring regions.

As the official Coimbatore Chapter of NAR India, we are part of the apex national organization representing the interests of real estate professionals across the country.

From just 4 founding members, COAREA has grown into a prestigious body of 50+ TNRERA-registered professionals, making it one of the most credible networks in the region.
