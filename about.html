About COAREA

The Official Coimbatore Chapter of NAR India

Setting the Blueprint for Excellence.

The Coimbatore Association of Realtors (COAREA) is a not-for-profit professional body dedicated to empowering, representing, and elevating real estate professionals across Coimbatore, Tirupur, Nilgiris, Erode, Salem, and neighbouring regions.

COAREA functions as the official Coimbatore Chapter of NAR India (National Association of Realtors – India), the apex national organization representing the interests of real estate professionals across the country.

Why Realtors Matter

Real estate transactions are among the most significant financial decisions individuals and businesses make. Professional realtors play a critical role in ensuring success.

Ethics & Transparency

Ensuring every transaction is fair, documented, and honest.

Market Insight

Accurate market data and pricing guidance for better decisions.

Legal Compliance

Navigating the complex world of RERA and regulatory requirements.

Risk Mitigation

Protecting the interests of buyers, sellers, and investors alike.

Our Journey

The Indian real estate sector experienced rapid expansion in the early 2010s. To address the lack of representation for professionals, NAR India was formed in 2008, followed by the establishment of COAREA in 2011.

From just 4 founding members, COAREA has grown into a prestigious body of 50+ TNRERA-registered professionals, including brokers, consultants, and property experts, making it the most credible network in the region.

Our Mission & Advocacy

COAREA is committed to building a professional, ethical, and sustainable real estate ecosystem.

Core Objectives

  • Protecting the interests of real estate brokers.
  • Acting as a collective voice before RERA and government bodies.
  • Promoting accountability and ethical business practices.

Industry Challenges We Tackle

  • Representing brokers in policy-making.
  • Managing increasing regulatory compliance.
  • Addressing delays or non-payment of commissions.

Leadership & Excellence
